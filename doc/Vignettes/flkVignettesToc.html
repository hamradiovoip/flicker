Flicker Vignettes - Short Examples

Vignettes are short examples that pose and answer questions of the form "how do I do xxxx?". We list the vignettes below. Each vignette describes the problem using demonstration data, the method suggested to solve the problem, any additional setups required, and links of related vignettes that may be referenced.
